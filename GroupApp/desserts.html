Recommendations

Chessecake
This is the perfect dessert for anyone with a desire for something sweet and creamy. Share it, or eat the whole thing yourself. You deserve it!
Lane Cake
Lane cake is a four-layer (though some prefer three) white cake with a thick bourbon-laced raisin filling. The egg whites are used for making the vanilla butter cake and the egg yolks for the custard filling.
Chocolate Brownies
This dessert is an all time favorite when it comes to American households. It's quick and easy to make, and the end result is amazing. Make sure you make two batches!

Sundae
The sundae is a sweet ice cream dessert. It typically consists of one or more scoops of ice cream topped with sauce or syrup, and in some cases other toppings including sprinkles, whipped cream, peanuts, maraschino cherries, or other fruits
Bread pudding
This lightly spiced bread pudding is made with simple pantry items like bread, eggs, milk, and sugar. Use a rich egg bread or a moist white loaf.
Gelato
Gelato is ice cream made in the Italian style.
