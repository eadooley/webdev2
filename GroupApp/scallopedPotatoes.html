Scalloped potatoes


Video

Ingredients


  1. 2 tablespoons butter.
  2. 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour.
  3. 1 teaspoon salt.
  4. 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
  5. 1-1/2 cups fat-free milk.
  6. 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese.
  7. 2 pounds red potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced (about 4 cups)
  8. 1 cup thinly sliced onions, divided.
