Chicken and Waffles


Video

Ingredients


  1. Vegetable oil, for shallow frying
  2. 1/4 cup hot sauce
  3. 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  4. 8 chicken tenders (about 1 pound)
  5. 3/4 cup instant flour (such as Wondra)
  6. 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  7. Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  8. 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  9. 2 scallions, sliced, plus more for garnish
  10. 1 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  11. 4 buttermilk or Belgian-style frozen waffles
  12. Maple syrup, for serving
