Chocolate Brownies


Video

Ingredients


  1. 200g dark chocolate
  2. 100g unsalted butter, very soft
  3. 250g caster sugar
  4. 4 large free range eggs, beaten to mix
  5. 1tsp vanilla essence
  6. 60g plain flour
  7. 60g cocoa powder
  8. 15cm square brownie tin or baking tin
