Recommendations

Chessecake
This is the perfect dessert for anyone with a desire for something sweet and creamy. Share it, or eat the whole thing. We know you deserve it!
Lane Cake
Lane cake is a four-layer (though some prefer three) white cake with a thick bourbon-laced raisin filling. The egg whites are used for making the vanilla butter cake and the egg yolks for the custard filling.
Chocolate Brownies
This dessert is an all time favorite when it comes to American households. It's quick and easy to make, and the end result is amazing. Make sure you make two batches!
