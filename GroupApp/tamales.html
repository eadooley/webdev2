Ingredients


  1. 3 1⁄2 lbs pork shoulder or 3 1⁄2 lbs pork butt, trimmed of fat and cut up
  2. 10 cups water
  3. 1 medium onion, quartered
  4. 3 garlic cloves, minced
  5. 3 1⁄2 teaspoons salt
  6. 4 cups red chili sauce (see Red Chili Sauce (To Be Used With Traditional Tamales) for red chili sauce)
  7. 3⁄4 cup shortening
  8. 6 cups masa harina
  9. 1 1⁄2 teaspoons baking powder
  10. 50 dried corn husks (about 8 inches long)
