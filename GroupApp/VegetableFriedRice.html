Vegetable Fried Rice


Video

Ingredients


  1. 2 tablespoon olive oil
  2. 1 small white onion, diced
  3. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 1/2 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  5. 1 package frozen mixed vegetables, cooked according to package instructions
  6. 4cups refrigerated cooked rice
  7. 3 eggs, scrambled
  8. 2 tablespoon soy sauce (or more to taste)
  9. 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  10. 2 teaspoon sesame oil
