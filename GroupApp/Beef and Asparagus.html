Ingredients


  1. 1 pound beef tenderloin or top sirloin steak, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  4. 1 tablespoon canola oil
  5. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  6. 1 green onion, sliced
  7. 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  8. 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
