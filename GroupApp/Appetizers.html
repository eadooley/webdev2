Recommendations

Bruchetta
Bruschetta is an antipasto from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese.
Crunchy Taco Cups
They’re layers of crunchy taco shell, seasoned beef, spicy tomatoes, and gooey cheese all in one perfect bite. You better make two servings because people are sure to be all over these.
Bacon Brown Sugar Smokies
These bacon wrapped smokies with brown sugar make a great appetizer! They are easy to make and can easily be made ahead of time & they freeze well!

Honey Buffalo Meatballs
The meatballs themselves are very tender, juicy, and flavorful from an easy group of spices. The spices make the ingredient list seem somewhat lengthy, but they just take seconds to throw in and take these meatballs all the way to the end zone; smother them in Honey Buffalo Sauce and you have a touchdown.
Beef-and-Asparagus
This is the perfect combination of meat and vegetables. Tie these bundles with a fresh chive to put the perfect touch on this edible package.
Stuffed Mushrooms
Stuffed mushrooms are a fabulous appetizer for any occasion. Start stuffing with these top-rated recipes using clams, sausage, crabs, and vegetarian options, too!
