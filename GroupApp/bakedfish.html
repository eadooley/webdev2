Ingredients


  1. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or to taste
  2. 2 pounds mackerel fillets
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  5. 1/4 cup butter, melted
  6. 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  7. 1/8 teaspoon ground paprika
