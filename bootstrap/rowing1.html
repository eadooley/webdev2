A Little Bit of Info

This is the most fun sport that I have ever been a part of. There are 9 total poeple in a boat, 8 rowers and one coxswain (they are the "captains" of the boat.) In an 8 boat, each person is only in charge of one oar, but in some 4, 2, and single boats you are in charge of 2 oars.

Rowing is fun

Races

In the sport of rowing races are called Regattas, and they are a full-day thing. You usually get there around 7 in the morning and don't leave until 5 in the evening. This can be pretty frustrating because you usually have only one or two races that take half an hour at most each.

Equipment

Rowing is a very expensive sport, and that is due to the price of the equipment. A single oar by itself is around $1,000 (keep in mind that there are eight of them.) The boat itself costs around $40,000, and that is definetly not cheap. If you drop one of those boats you pretty much drop 2 years of your college.
